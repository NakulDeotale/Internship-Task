Hypothesis and Findings:
1. Older families have moderate spending habits as they usually tend to have secure, stress free, healthy and stable life in old age.
   They believe in simple lifestyle as they have fixed income, savings and very low desires for materialistic things.

2. Younger families tend to buy premium products as they want to gain popularity, experience, create brand image in society, and live their dreams.
   Also they have stable income to spend on such premium products.

3. Older singles/couples want to cherish the happy moments in life and make it memorable, which motivates them to buy premium product.

4. Young singles/couples especially in their teenage mostly prefer mainstream products due to unstability in finances.
